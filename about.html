WHO WE ARE

About Us

Ashanut Initiative for Africa is a non-governmental, not for profit community-based organization founded with a sole purpose to support women, children and youth in the rural communities through education sponsorship, economic development to ensure sustainable livelihood, increasing access to affordable renewable energy, improved reproductive health and HIV/AIDs awareness.

Our Vision

We envision a self-sustainable community with equitable service delivery in Teso region.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a platform to build partnership among all sectors in the community through efficient and effective service delivery, and to ensure that all stakeholders act in unison with the common good of the people of Teso region.
