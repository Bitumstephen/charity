Giving is Virtue.

when we keep the practice of Virtue at the heart of your everyday life, We live with purpose.

Living with purpose is a noble pursuit. One way to infuse purpose into our daily lives is by practicing virtue, especially the act of giving. By making giving a regular part of our lives, we not only make a positive impact on the world around us but also cultivate a sense of fulfillment and joy within ourselves.

So let us strive to make giving a habit and embody the virtues that make us better individuals. By doing so, we not only bring meaning and purpose to our own lives but also make the world a better place, one act of kindness at a time.

Shelter and Livelihood

Poverty rates in Uganda sub-counties rate from 15% to more than 60% of the population. We empower communities in rural areas with agriculture to ensure increased production of food enough for household and sale to generate income for other basic needs.
